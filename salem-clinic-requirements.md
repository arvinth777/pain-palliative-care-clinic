## Design Specifications

### Color Palette
- **Primary Blue:** #2563eb (rich, professional blue)
- **Accent Blue:** #1e40af (darker blue for depth)
- **Light Blue:** #60a5fa (hover states, accents)
- **Cream/Off-white:** #fefce8 or #fef3c7 (section backgrounds)
- **White:** #ffffff (main background)
- **Text Dark:** #1f2937 (body text)
- **Text Gray:** #6b7280 (secondary text)

### Design Principles
- **Aesthetic:** Modern, clean, compassionate, professional
- **Target Audience:** Middle-aged to elderly pain sufferers, cancer patients
- **User Experience:** Easy navigation, clear CTAs, minimal cognitive load
- **Accessibility:** WCAG 2.1 AA compliant
- **Visual Hierarchy:** Clear, with ample whitespace

### Typography
- **Headings:** Inter, Poppins, or similar modern sans-serif
- **Body Text:** Open Sans or system fonts for readability
- **Minimum Font Sizes:** 16px body text (consider older demographic)
- **Line Height:** 1.6-1.8 for readability

### Responsive Breakpoints
- **Mobile:** 320px - 640px
- **Tablet:** 641px - 1024px
- **Desktop:** 1025px and above

  - Normal text: 4.5:1 minimum
  - Large text: 3:1 minimum
- Focus indicators on interactive elements
- Alt text for all images
- Semantic HTML (proper heading hierarchy)
- Form labels properly associated
- Skip to main content link
- No reliance on color alone for information

- Use Lighthouse accessibility audit
- Test with screen reader (NVDA, JAWS)
- Keyboard-only navigation test

## Content Tone & Voice

### Guidelines
- **Compassionate yet Professional:** Balance expertise with empathy
- **Patient-Friendly Language:** Avoid excessive medical jargon
- **Reassuring Tone:** Address patient anxieties, especially about procedures
- **Clear and Direct:** Easy to understand for all education levels
- **Emphasize Experience:** 30 years expertise, but not boastful
- **Focus on Outcomes:** Patient quality of life, pain relief

### Example Tone

❌ **Bad:** "We provide pain management services utilizing interventional modalities."

✅ **Good:** "We understand that living with chronic pain affects every aspect of your life. With 30 years of experience, Dr. Kirupakaran offers compassionate, personalized care using advanced techniques to help you reclaim your quality of life."

❌ **Bad:** "Ultrasound-guided procedures available."

✅ **Good:** "Our state-of-the-art ultrasound equipment allows Dr. Kirupakaran to see exactly where the needle goes during injections, making procedures safer, more accurate, and more comfortable for you."
