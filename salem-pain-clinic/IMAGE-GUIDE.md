## üé® **Photo Guidelines**

### **Best Practices:**
- **Resolution**: At least 1200px wide for best quality
- **Format**: JPG for photos, PNG for logos
- **Lighting**: Natural or bright artificial light
- **Focus**: Clear, not blurry
- **Staging**: Clean, organized, professional
- **Privacy**: No patient faces or identifiable info

### **What to Avoid:**
- ‚ùå Blurry or dark photos
- ‚ùå Cluttered or messy backgrounds
- ‚ùå Patient information visible
- ‚ùå Low resolution images
- ‚ùå Extreme angles

## üì± **How to Take Good Photos**

### **Equipment Photos:**
1. Clean equipment first
2. Use good lighting
3. Take from front angle
4. Show full device clearly
5. Avoid shadows

### **Room Photos:**
1. Clean and organize first
2. Use wide angle if possible
3. Show entire space
4. Good lighting essential
5. Remove personal items

### **Doctor Photo:**
1. Professional attire (white coat)
2. Clean background
3. Good posture
4. Natural smile
5. Eye contact with camera
